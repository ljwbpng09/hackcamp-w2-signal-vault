# Narrative Assets (v2)

## 中文版（原版）

### Product Name
AI Blackbox

### Tagline
先签字，再计分

### One-liner
给赛中交易员和盘口研究员：AI Blackbox 先在结果前写下判断，再自动结算对错，输出人人可独立审计的预测履历。

### Pitch 60s（v2）
前 10 秒：这是给赛中交易员和盘口研究员用的——比赛进行中盘口会瞬间跳动，他们只有几秒决定跟不跟。  
接下来：AI Blackbox 扮演一个 Matchday Scout，自动发现当日比赛盘，也能用 `/add england` 现场加市场。  
但真正关键不是“发现得快”，而是“必须先签字”。每次判断上涨或下跌，它都在结果前留下公开记录。10 分钟后系统自动结算，Dashboard 实时更新胜率。  
这和普通提醒工具的区别只有一句：我们先承诺，再评分，且不能改答案。  
如果 AI 不能在结果前签字，它就不配谈准确率。

## English Version (Rewritten)

### Product Name
**Ai Blackbox**

### Tagline
Commit first. Score later.

### One-liner
Built for live traders and market researchers, **Ai Blackbox** commits each call before results and grades itself ten minutes later.

### Pitch 60s
Imagine you're trading live during a packed match night.  
Odds jump hard. You get seconds to decide.  
Most tools send alerts. Then they rewrite history later.  
**Ai Blackbox** works like a matchday scout.  
It finds tonight’s markets automatically.  
You can add one instantly with `/add england`.  
Now the key part: it commits the call before outcome.  
Up or down, locked in, no edits.  
Ten minutes later, it grades the call in public.  
That record updates live for everyone to inspect.  
If an AI won’t sign first, accuracy means nothing.  
So here’s the real question: what happens when it gets the biggest call wrong?
